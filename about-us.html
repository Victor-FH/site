about us

Our Landscape Designs is founded in 2013 in London. We encompasses a wide and continuously growing portfolio of garden designs from classic to contemporary, coastal gardens, rural landscaping and country gardens, as well as London and Copenhagen courtyards and rooftops. We also design functional outdoor spaces for corporate clients.

nete højlund

dip - garden design

nete@ourlandscapedesigns.com

I am originally an interior designer, and I have always been attracted to the idea of extending interior design into exterior spaces, viewing them as a whole.

I was awarded KLC Design of the Year in 2010, followed by the RHS Silver Gilt at the Hampton Court Flower Show in 2011. I co-founded the garden design practice, which has since gained international recognition.

burcu phung

dip - garden design

I am originally from Istanbul and earned my diploma in garden design from KLC School of Design in London. My passion for architecture and love of nature are reflected in my work.

I find great joy in my work, as it allows me to channel my enthusiasm into plans that come to life as the gardens are developed.
